# POS GLASSES Optic Bridge Logo Design

## Objective

Create a premium, modern logo asset for the POS GLASSES optical-store point-of-sale system. The identity must work in the app sidebar, printed receipts, browser favicon, and future retail materials.

## Brand Position

POS GLASSES is an operational retail system for selling and managing eyewear. It should feel clear, fast, trustworthy, and contemporary rather than like a generic SaaS product or a fashion-only optical brand.

## Selected Direction: Optic Bridge

The mark is a reduced pair of optical lenses joined by a single rounded bridge. The bridge contains a subtle forward scan or checkout path, connecting the visual act of seeing with the operational act of selling. It remains recognizable as a small app icon without relying on a literal sunglasses illustration, money symbol, or price tag.

The wordmark uses the exact text `POS GLASSES` in a bold, modern sans-serif style with modest tracking. The icon and wordmark form a horizontal lockup for navigation and a stacked or icon-only variant for small spaces.

## Visual System

- Primary dark: `#10201D` (existing sidebar tone)
- Primary teal: `#0F766E` (existing checkout accent)
- Supporting mint: `#8CE1D6`
- Light neutral: `#F3F6F8`
- Mark style: flat, geometric, rounded terminals, high contrast, no gradients required
- Avoid: literal sunglasses, price-tag silhouettes, dollar signs, excessive shine, generic AI sparkle marks, fine details, and tiny unreadable copy

## Deliverables

1. A premium 3 × 3 brand-kit overview board showing the selected Optic Bridge concept, construction, palette, type direction, and practical POS applications.
2. A standalone square logo mark with a clean chroma-key background, locally converted to a transparent PNG.
3. The final project asset saved non-destructively under `frontend/assets/images/`.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The mark reads as a modern optical-retail identity at 32 px and above.
- It uses the existing teal and dark green system consistently.
- It is visually simple enough to reproduce as a future vector asset.
- The background is truly transparent in the delivered PNG.
- The final asset does not overwrite existing project assets.

## Validation

Inspect the generated board and standalone mark for symbol clarity, spelling of `POS GLASSES`, balanced proportions, unexpected text, chroma-key fringe, alpha channel, and transparent corner pixels.
